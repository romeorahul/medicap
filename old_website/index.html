The Globally performing High Choice Cap for PP Infusion Bottles.  
Caps produced by Medicap Inc. are the closures for IV Bottles produced by ISBM and Preform Stretch Blow moulded ( two step ) method. It is globally preferred for the unique design with ease of operation, and safe packing. The PP Caps for Infusion Bottles are manufactured by Medicap Inc using fully Automated Process and under Clean Room environment.  
The Caps are moulded with Certified Polypropylene Produced specifically for IV fluids application by the world best companies.  
No contamination and tempering is possible using three piece Medicap produced PP Cap for Infusion bottles.
 

Advantages

1 Packed in Clean Room and hence directly usable.
