About Us




Corporate Profile

Medicap Inc. is a young and dynamic company dedicated towards manufacturing of closures for pharmaceutical industry particularly, Euro Head caps. The driving force of our business is the thorough knowledge about the product and end user requirements.

Our experience of more than 30 Years in the industry backed with a high end technology has resulted in the evolution of Medicap Inc. We started operations in 2014 by setting up a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility laced with the latest technology and have a large volume production capacity which makes us different from other manufacturers.

We are situated at Vadodara in the state of Gujarat with major sea-port within the vicinity of 140-400 kms.


Our Vision

To Become a preferred business associate for all leading pharmaceutical companies mainly Intravenous fluid manufacturers.

Our Mission

To Design, Manufacture and Distribute clinically relevant, state-of-the-art and best in class pharmaceutical closures.

Clean Room Production...

Our Caps are used for injectables and hence utmost care and precaution is taken during the entire manufacturing process. The Caps are automatically injection moulded and assembled in class 10,000 (class 7).

Our ultra-modern facility is built on rock solid GMP guidelines. The total Quality System covers raw material controls and finished product inspection to ensure synonymous quality at each stage of production.
