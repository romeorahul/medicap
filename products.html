Our Products



We manufacture Euro Head Caps which are closures for plastic Infusion bottles made from Blow-Fill-Seal Technology and Stretch Blow Moulding with Wash-Fill-Seal technology. By investing in a state-of-the-art production unit, we are setting a new benchmark in manufacturing of Euro Head caps in India.

The large size, 72 cavity moulds with hot runner technology, tailor made injection moulding machine and ultra high speed fully automated assembly machine ensure product quality without human interference. Moulding and Assembly process is carried out in a clean room condition.

Our In-house laboratory facility and Random Testing process ensures a defect free product. This process also includes the testing of Pull load of the pull ring and Auto clave test.
