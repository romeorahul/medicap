Medicap Inc -
The Evolution

Our experience of more than 30 Years in the industry backed with a high end technology has resulted in the evolution of Medicap Inc. We started operations in 2014 by setting up a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility laced with the latest technology and have a large volume production capacity which makes us different from other manufacturers.

We are situated at Vadodara in the state of Gujarat with major sea-port within the vicinity of 140-400 kms.

Corporate Profile.

Medicap Inc. is a young and dynamic company dedicated towards manufacturing of closures for pharmaceutical industry particularly, Euro Head caps. The driving force of our business is the thorough knowledge about the product and end user requirements.
